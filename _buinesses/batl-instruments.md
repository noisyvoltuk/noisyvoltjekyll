The Golden Whisk has been a cornerstone of Market Street since 2012, when founder Sarah Chen left her London restaurant career to open a neighbourhood bakery rooted in proper technique and local ingredients.

Every loaf of sourdough is made with a 10-year-old starter, cold-proved overnight, and baked in a deck oven each morning. The pastry counter changes with the seasons — expect blood orange tarts in January, strawberry mille-feuille in June, and salted caramel choux all year round.

## What we offer

- **Bread** — sourdough, rye, focaccia, and seasonal specials baked daily
- **Pastries** — croissants, pain au chocolat, Danish, and rotating tarts
- **Café** — specialty espresso, batch filter, and a short seasonal food menu
- **Catering** — platters, celebration cakes, and corporate breakfast boxes (48hr notice)

## Allergen information

Full allergen information is available in-store and on our website. We can accommodate many dietary requirements with advance notice — please call or email ahead.
